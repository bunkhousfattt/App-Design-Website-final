About INSPIRED

INSPIRED is a daily drawing challenge. Every day a new prompt word is chosen and artists of all different levels can share their interpretation of it.

Upload your artwork each day to create your streak, discover other artists on the Explore tab, and follow friends to see their daily work.

Made by artists, for artists to forget the social media bull****, and just keep making art!

How it works
  • A new word is revealed each day at midnight
  • Upload your art before the day ends
  • Browse Explore to see everyone elses take on the prompt
  • Follow artists whose work you love
