About INSPIRED

INSPIRED is a daily drawing challenge community. Every day, a new prompt word is chosen — and artists of all levels share their interpretation of it.

Upload your artwork each day to build your streak, discover other artists on Explore, and follow friends to see their daily work in your Friends feed.

No likes. No algorithms. No pressure — just art, every day.

How it works
  • A new word is revealed each day at midnight
  • Upload your drawing before the day ends
  • Browse Explore to see everyone's take on the prompt
  • Follow artists whose work you love
